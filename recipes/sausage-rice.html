Chinese Sausage Rice

Description

Create a delicious Chinese Sausage Rice, also known as "lap cheong fan" in Cantonese, effortlessly with just a rice cooker and a few ingredients. This straightforward recipe delivers a satisfying meal that perfectly balances the flavors of rice and savory sausages with minimal fuss.

Ingredients

  • jasmine rice
  • water
  • Chinese sausage (lap cheong)

Steps

  1. Place the rice into your rice cooker, then fill it with water up to the marker that corresponds to the number of cups of rice you've added.
  2. Add the Chinese sausage into the rice cooker pot and switch on the rice cooker. It will steam the sausages together with the rice.
  3. Slice the sausage and serve over the rice.
