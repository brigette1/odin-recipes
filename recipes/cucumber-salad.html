Asian Cucumber Salad

Description

The Asian cucumber salad offers delightful and revitalizing flavors, making it an ideal appetizer and an excellent choice for hot weather.

Ingredients

  • seedless cucumbers
  • sesame oil
  • salt
  • sugar
  • light soy sauce
  • rice vinegar
  • garlic
  • chili oil
  • toasted sesame seeds
  • chopped cilantro

Steps

  1. Rinse the cucumbers and gently dry them using a fresh towel. Create the salad dressing by mixing together salt, sugar, sesame oil, light soy sauce, and rice vinegar until the sugar and salt have fully dissolved. Put it aside for later use.
  2. Slice the cucumbers into bite-sized pieces, approximately 1/4 inch thick.
  3. In a bowl, combine the cucumbers with the dressing, minced garlic, and chili oil. Mix thoroughly. Garnish the dish with cilantro and sesame seeds and serve!
