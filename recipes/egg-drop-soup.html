Egg Drop Soup

Description

Egg drop soup is a cherished Chinese comfort dish known for its smooth and silky texture, and delightful savory taste. This recipe, which takes just 15 minutes to prepare, is straightforward and simple to follow.

Ingredients

  • chicken stock
  • sesame oil
  • salt
  • sugar
  • white pepper
  • turmeric
  • cornstarch
  • eggs
  • scallion
  • MSG
