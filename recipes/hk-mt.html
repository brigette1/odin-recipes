Hong Kong Milk Tea

Description

Indulge in the rich and aromatic flavors of Hong Kong milk tea with this recipe. This beloved beverage combines black tea and creamy condensed milk for a perfectly balanced and satisfying brew that captures the essence of Hong Kong's tea culture.

Ingredients

  • hot water
  • black tea leaves
  • evaporated milk
  • sweetened condensed milk

Steps

  1. Place 6 cups of hot water into a pot. Add the tea and bring it to a boil. Then, reduce the heat and allow it to simmer for 15 minutes.
  2. Pour the tea into a mesh strainer.
  3. Transfer the brewed tea into a cup, and mix in evaporated milk and sweetened condensed milk.
  4. Stir thoroughly and enjoy!
